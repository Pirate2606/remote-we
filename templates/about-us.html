About Remote-WE

The superior quality of Green Grocery with superior ambience

Farmers now-a-days are not getting a proper price for their crops and labour due to the gap between them and the seller. They are selling it to the retailers at a very low rate due to which they are not even getting a profit which are leading to more unpaid loans from the bank which ultimately leads to poverty and suicides by the farmers and their family.

Due to this increasing number of suicides by farmers due to the middleman between them and the consumer we have thought of an idea to connect the farmer directly with the customers. We have made an application through which the farmers will get maximum profit and can sell their vegetables online. It is a fully functional website where the users can register themselves and they can also select a prime membership for faster delivery and other cashback offers. It is a socio startup to bridge the gap between farmers and the buyers by connecting them directly without any middleman.
